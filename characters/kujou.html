CHARACTER

Kujou Sara

Kujou Sara is a playable Electro character in Genshin Impact. A tengu, Sara is the adopted daughter of the Kujou Clan of the Tenryou Commission. Loyal to both her clan and the Shogun, Sara carries out her orders by the Shogun's will.
